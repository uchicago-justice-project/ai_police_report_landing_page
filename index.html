AI Generated Police Reports

Across the United States, Police Departments are starting to use AI to automate report writing. Police incident reports are often used as evidence in legal proceedings where a single mistake could have huge implications. Knowing that Large Language Models can often hallucinate and stretch the truth we wondered if AI Police Report Writing Tools could be trusted with this important task.

In 2023, Calvin Riley Sr was pulled over and arrested for a DUI. The human written police report claimed that an open bottle of vodka was found in the car, but body cam footage later showed the police officer, Kiersten Oliver, opened the bottle herself, dumped the contents and then put the empty bottle back into the car.
